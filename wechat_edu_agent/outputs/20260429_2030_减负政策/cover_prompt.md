# Cover Prompt

cover_prompt_cn: 一位疲惫的小学生坐在堆满课本的书桌前，窗外是模糊的城市天际线，书桌一角放着一部手机，屏幕上显示‘拍照打卡’任务被划掉；家长的手从画面外伸入，轻轻放在孩子肩头，表情焦虑而坚定；整体色调灰蓝与暖黄对比，突出冲突感；风格写实，光影柔和，避免恐怖或夸张。
cover_prompt_en: A tired elementary school student sits at a desk piled with textbooks, with a blurred city skyline outside the window; on the desk corner, a smartphone screen shows a crossed-out 'photo check-in' task; a parent's hand reaches gently onto the child's shoulder from outside the frame, expression anxious yet determined; overall color palette contrasts cool gray-blue with warm yellow to highlight tension; realistic style with soft lighting, avoiding horror or exaggeration.
negative_prompt: 恐怖、低俗、夸张、血腥、暴力、名人形象、真实学校logo、机构logo、卡通化、抽象艺术、水印、文字遮挡主体。
suggested_layout: 竖版构图，主体居中，书桌和手机在前景，孩子和家长在后景，标题文字置于上方空白区域，留白约30%用于封面文字。
cover_text: 减负减的是形式，不是竞争
